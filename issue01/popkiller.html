HOW TO BE A POPKILLER

Some people may peruse this k-rad website and wonder to themselves, "what can I do to be like those guys? I wanna be a POP-KILLER!" Until now, you probably didn't even know you wanted it, but damnit, now you want it. The best way to go about this is by making a list of goals and working at them one at a time until one day, in the distant future, you find yourself emotionally damaged and embittered toward the entire world. You, the uninitiated may not have the wisdom and insight to make such a list, but here at KP, we do, so we made one for you. Here it is:


<DISCLAIMER> The people at Kill Pop and Stylishpants.org are not responsible for the bad things that will happen if anyone follows or interprets as factual information any advice or proclamations of any kind. This applies doubly to advice and proclamations found on this page or any other associated with the Kill Pop site. As in all cases where one is presented with information, you have to use judgement; if you are not able to use judgement in day to day situations or you judge yourself unable to judge the things you will soon read as right or wrong, then you should exercise your judgement and judge that you ought to be reading some other page that normal people wrote. This is the realm of the desparate, depraved and dastardly. If you are none of these things, watch out.


  1. 1. The first thing to do if you are a frequent reader of this page and you want to be like us is to obtain a plain black shirt and some white paint (whether you use normal, a more practical or decidedly more nefarious means of obtaining things is left completely up to yourself). Then you take your paint and write 'Kill Pop' on your shirt. This shirt is your badge of honour, wear it when you have nothing else clean. Now that you have the base KP shirt you can produce more shirts in the same manner. Although you should be creative, here are some suggested slogans to show the world you're a weirdo:

    • Kill fig-newtons

    • Break Shit

    • I like to eat Spice-Girls

    • Blow Shit Up

    • I Throw Rocks at Statues

    • I Like Pointy Things


  1. The next thing to do is to learn to tell people that you don't like them. Our new revolution will be founded on honesty, and in an honest world everyone deserves to know exactly what you think of them. Beating around the bush by being rude to somone is obsolete; tell them you don't like them, they leave you alone. This simple equation is too often forgotten and people we don't like irritate us for too long without being properly disuaded. However, the power to use language properly should be used wisely and a few guidelines should be kept in mind when your god-like potence is brought to bear: never tell someone you dislike them in anger, you could burn your bridges with someone you actually do like; don't tell someone you dislike them if you think the situation might change, this is when just being a little rude is appropriate; don't laugh or be mean when you say it, an honest and unsarcastic affirmation allows for no mistakes and sometimes keeps a lack of affection from degenerating into hatred.

  2. Separating yourself from the rest of society is the next step. There are no real actions to take, merely the aquisition of the attitude of an outcast and the real feeling of abandonment. This is easier than it sounds: too often people who are not actually losers go around telling people that they are dark and mysterious, and therefore like the cool people at Kill Pop and Stylish Pants. They are not like us. If you do not already feel this way the best thing to do is to move to a city where you don't know anyone, then look out the window and think about how alone you are. This works even better if you have no friends or family to talk to, but this is sometimes hard to muster, so I might forgive you if you can't.

  3. Nextly, you have to bottle up everything. No venting of any sort for a long time is the only way you can possibly hope to match the ball of latent rage that is a POP-KILLER. After you feel angry enough, you are allowed to vent, but only allow a loss of control on inappropriate things; it's ok to get mad at stuff you're allowed to get mad at, but only get a little mad. Then go screaming ape-shit over bumping into inanimate objects and stupid things you do to yourself.

  4. Next, you write. Since no one wants to talk to you any more and you can't stand to have a whole conversation with stupid people, you will have to rely on writing angry monologues for the internet. They should be long winded, sarcastic, and completely honest in a way that conventional media cannot be. You don't need to pull punches on the internet because people will stop reading if they're offended. Plus, if they are offended, you can't see them cry and they can't kick your ass, which is good, since others crying around might lead to some uncomfortable emotion on your part, and asskickings suck.

  5. The final thing you have to do is get a goal in life. In light of the other things to do, this might seem strange, but it's not, as not just any goal will do. The goal must be something that is both unattainable and probably not all that satisfying when attained. Then you must tell yourself that you are working toward this goal, even though you are doing nothing of the sort, like writing angry web pages. Now you keep this up for a few years until the soul has been sucked completely from your body and all that remains is a wasteland of self pity and regret with this little goal glowing in the background.


Do all of these things and you will be a POP-KILLER of the 12th degree. What this accomplishes? Our brave explorers of the understimulated are still trying to find out.


Oh yeah, being heinously jaded, or at least amused by the wrong things, helps too.
