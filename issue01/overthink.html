OVERTHINKUNDERACT

There's a problem... or a phenomenon that's been identified as a problem, anyway. We've all been told that to act without thinking is a bad thing... of course, there's some flaws in that argument, as a general argument, but I'm concerned with only the extreme case: to think without action, or to allow one's thinking to interefere with the success of one's actions.

We (er, if you're cool, you know who we are - if you're really cool, you're one of us) call this phenomenon OVERTHINKUNDERACT - a big nasty word, designed to confuse the brain into freaking out before it can really realize what to do about it.

What the hell does that mean? You're right... we need to go deeper. Why do you think it is, that all the assholes are getting the women? Hm? Is it 'cause women like assholes? - no, not really. There's a bunch of theories involving confidence, and they work rather well, but I'm worried about mojo here. You see, if you're in the groove, you'll get the bananas.

You're always in the groove - or a groove - but some grooves have rotten bananas. Grooves host to rotten bananas are known as ruts - "rut" being (in this case) short for "rotten" (wholly because I said so - QED) - it's a rotten groove - a rut.

Whatever it is you want, given a system of grooves and ruts, it's not a rotten banana. That's how the health of the banana is determined in the first place... what you want is a healthy ripe banana.

Ergo, you've got to be in the right groove (or the groove) or you'll get rotton bananas, which you don't want. The problem is, the ruts suck. Literally. They suck you in... you must be in motion or you're headed for a rut. If you're out of motion for too long, you'll end up in a rut. As we all know, ruts take a lot of energy to get out of.

Overthinking, or underacting will, obviously, get you into a rut. I'm not going to worry about overacting, since you're parents have already warned you that jumping into a deep well is a bloody stupid thing to do. Unless you can find some good bananas down there, of course. Bananas make you strong, and give you superpowers, such that you can scale walls and stuff... but I digress.

So, anyway, assholes getting all the hotties... doesn't that piss you off? Okay, here's the breakdown. Play by play. Asshole wants a hottie. He hits on her - right away. Why? 'Cause he's a big dumb jock and he's not bright enough to think about it.

So... asshole gravitates towards the groove. You, what are you doing. You're thinking about it... you're worrying about rejection, or perhaps about being creepy, or maybe... oh, I don't know... the point is, you're thinking about it, 'cause you're a nice guy, eh? Well... let's look at the scoreboard, you're getting sucked into a rut, and Mr. Hole himself is getting in the groove.

What's the deal here? Well, given this trend, you'll end up in a rut - whining to your friends that you never get any dates, while Mr. Asshole is with the hottie. He's got the good bananas. Yours stink, man.

Let's look at this conventionally... how does this relate to things people can accept literally? Well... he tried, you just sat there and sipped on your club soda. The least you could have done was gotten plastered enough that you stopped thinking, and were left to act - or pass out.

I guess the point is that action begets action, and inaction begets a subscription to a stroke magazine.
